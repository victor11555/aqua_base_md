# Deleting a profile — База знаний Aqua Delivery

To delete an account in the app, you need to follow a few steps:

Step 1. Go to the «Profile» section in the lower menu  
![](https://howto.aqua-delivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Рисунок1-5.jpg)

Step 2. Click on «Profile» again  
![](https://howto.aqua-delivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Рисунок1-6.jpg)

Step 3. Click on “My Data”  
![](https://howto.aqua-delivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Рисунок3.jpg)

Step 4. Select “Delete Account”

![](https://howto.aqua-delivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Рисунок4.jpg)

Step 5. Confirm the account deletion

![](https://howto.aqua-delivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Рисунок5.jpg)

**Note:**  
**After deleting the profile, it can be restored only through the operator.**
